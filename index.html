Frequently Asked Questions

How does Email Generator Pro work?

Email Generator Pro uses advanced AI technology (Mistral) to generate professional emails based on your specifications. Simply select the email type, tone, length, and language, then describe your email purpose. The AI will create a polished, professional email in seconds.

Is Email Generator Pro free to use?

Yes, Email Generator Pro is completely free to use with no sign-up required. You can generate professional emails in 12 different languages without any limitations.

What languages does Email Generator Pro support?

We support 12 languages: English, French, Spanish, German, Italian, Portuguese, Arabic, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Russian, and Dutch. The AI adapts grammar and cultural conventions for each language.

Can I reply to emails using Email Generator Pro?

Yes! Email Generator Pro has a dedicated Reply mode where you can paste a received email and select your reply intent (accept, decline, clarify, etc.). The AI will generate an appropriate, contextual response that maintains the conversation flow.

Is my data safe and private?

Absolutely. We do not store any of your emails or personal information. All email generation happens in real-time, and no data is saved on our servers. Your privacy is our top priority.

What is the Auto Spell Check feature?

The Auto Spell Check & Polish feature uses AI to proofread your email, fixing spelling errors, grammar mistakes, and improving sentence flow. It ensures your email is professional and error-free before you send it.

Can I edit the generated emails?

Yes! After generating an email, you can click the "Edit" button to make any changes you want. You can also regenerate the email if you'd like a completely different version with the same settings.

What email types can I generate?

We support many email types including: Business, Marketing, Follow-up, Thank You, Apology, Request, Introduction, Invitation, Newsletter, Cold Outreach, and a general "Other" category for any type of email.

Why do you ask for gender information?

Gender information helps the AI use correct grammar and appropriate salutations in languages that have gender-specific forms. This is especially important for languages like French, Spanish, German, and Arabic.

Do I need to create an account?

No, you don't need to create an account or sign up. Just visit the website and start generating emails immediately. It's completely hassle-free.
